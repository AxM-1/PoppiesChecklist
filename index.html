AirBnB Checklist 91A

  • - Change bedding and replace with new fresh linen
    - Clean all tables and desks in the room
    - All headboards of the beds need a quick quip free from any dust
    - Change the bins
    - Hoover and mop the area

  • - Clean all bathroom fittings
    - Wash basin needs to be cleaned and dried; check for any limescale and try scrub them off
    - Basin Cabinet needs to be inspected for cleanliness
    - Taps need cleaning and buffing, leaving them shiny with no water residue
    - Toilets need cleaning inside and outside; toilet seat needs to be cleaned and wiped dry
    - Shower screen must be streak-free, and any limescale or watermarks need to be removed
    - Shower drain needs to be opened and cleaned
    - Radiator must be wiped down, leaving it polished
    - Replace toilet roll with a new one (Attention: never leave an old roll)
    - Put one single hand towel in the bathroom
    - Hoover and mop the bathroom

  • - Clean all countertops and cabinets, leaving them streak-free
    - Clean the hob, ensuring there are no food spills left
    - Clean the sink and all dishes; dry them and place them back in the cabinets
    - Check TEA, SUGAR, and COFFEE; make sure there is sufficient
    - Empty the kettle and wipe it from outside
    - Remove toaster trays and give them a good shake and wipe them clean
    - Empty the fridge if necessary and clean the inside and outside
    - Clean all sitting area tables and benches
    - Hoover and mop the whole kitchen

  • - Hoover and Mop Hallway and stairs

  • - Make sure all room windows are closed before leaving
    - Make sure all doors are locked properly
    - Make sure to take out trash and dump it in the designated area
    - Lock the main door, wait 20 seconds, and double-check
